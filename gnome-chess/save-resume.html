Save and resume

Saving your game

You can save the game and load it at any time. To save:

  1. Click Save the current game.

  2. Select a location for your game. It is best to save it in a location you will easily remember for future retrieval.

  3. Click Save.

Resuming your game

To resume a previously saved game:

  1. Click Open a saved game.

  2. Select the file for your saved game.

  3. Click Open.
