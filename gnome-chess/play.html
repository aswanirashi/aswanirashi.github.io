Play Chess

Chess displays the main game window at the start:

The game is played by moving chess pieces using the mouse. Each player alternates to move a piece in the appropriate colour, from one board position to another valid board position. To move a chess piece:

  1. Click on the desired chess piece to move. You can see the possible squares highlighted, where the chess piece can move.

  2. Click on the square where you want to place the chess piece.
