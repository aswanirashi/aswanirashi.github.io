Play Swell Foop

The board starts as a full grid of objects depending on the size of board you select. You can remove the tiles as follows:

  • If a group of objects are adjacent and all the same type, then they can be removed simply by clicking them or by pressing the space bar.

  • Once the group has been removed the objects above them fall down to fill the space. If an entire column is cleared then the objects slide leftward to fill the gap.
