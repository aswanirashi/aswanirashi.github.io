Puzzles

Puzzles are divided into three categories namely, HuaRong Trail, Challenge Pack and Skill Pack based on the difficulty level of the game. While the first and the last each contains nine puzzles, the second one provides ten beautiful puzzles to the user.

Navigate among puzzles

There are a set of nineteen amazing puzzles, to browse among the puzzles:

  1. Click on View Puzzles in the bottom right, choose a category. All the puzzles in the category will be displayed.

  2. You can navigate sequentially among the puzzles with the Next Puzzle and Previous Puzzle.
