Moves

The consecutive moves of a block are considered to be a single move. For example, moving a block up one square then right two squares is considered one move.

Goal piece moves

The goal location may be positioned slightly off the board. The goal piece, which is the patterned block, is always permitted to move to it. However, no other blocks are permitted to move off the board.
